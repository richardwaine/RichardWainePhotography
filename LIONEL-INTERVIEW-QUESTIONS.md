# Interview Questions for Lionel Adriano

**Purpose:** Blog interview to showcase Lionel's expertise, build topical authority for makeup content, and highlight the team behind Richard Waine Photography.

---

## Background & Story

1. For people who don't know you yet, tell me a little about yourself and how you got into makeup artistry.

2. You're originally from Hawaii. What brought you to Lancaster, Pennsylvania?

3. Was there a specific moment or experience that made you realize makeup was your calling?

4. You're a certified M.A.C. makeup artist. What did that training teach you that you still use today?

5. You've been voted best makeup artist in Lancaster County multiple times. What do you think sets your work apart?

## Revolve Atelier & Business

6. Tell me about Revolve Atelier. What's the vision behind your business?

7. What types of clients do you typically work with outside of headshot photography?

8. How do you balance the creative/artistic side of makeup with the business side?

## Makeup Philosophy & Approach

9. How would you describe your makeup philosophy?

10. What's the biggest misconception people have about professional makeup?

11. When a client sits in your chair, what's the first thing you assess or consider?

## Headshot Makeup Specifically
