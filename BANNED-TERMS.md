# Banned Terms & Phrases

**Never use these terms or phrases in any content. They are overused, meaningless, or AI tells.**

---

## Overused Business Clichés

1. **"First impressions"** - Overused, diluted, meaningless. Nobody knows what it means anymore.
2. **"Based on X years of experience"** / **"In my X years..."** - Nobody cares in AI overviews. Just answer the question.
3. **"Game-changer"** - Empty buzzword
4. **"Revolutionary"** - Empty buzzword without substance
5. **"Cutting-edge"** - Empty buzzword without substance
6. **"Innovative"** - Empty buzzword without substance

---

## AI Tell Phrases

7. **"For [topic]..."** as section opener (e.g., "For headshots...")
8. **"before you ever..."** (e.g., "before you ever walk into a room")
9. **"in the [X] environment/landscape/world/market"** - Classic AI construction
10. **"In today's world"** / **"In this day and age"** / **"In the modern era"**
11. **"It's important to note"** / **"It's worth noting"** / **"Needless to say"**
12. **"The fact is"** / **"The truth is"**
13. **"Moreover"** / **"Furthermore"** / **"Additionally"** - As paragraph starters

---

## Prohibited Punctuation Patterns

14. **Em dashes (—)** - Used as pause devices
15. **Semicolons (;)** - Used to show sophistication
16. **Colon-based two-part sentences** - Used repeatedly

---

## Prescriptive Language (Use Sparingly)

17. **"You should"** - Too prescriptive, use "tends to work" or "often works well"
18. **"You must"** - Too prescriptive
19. **"You need to"** - Too prescriptive

---

21. **"Leverage"**
22. **"Harness"**
23. **"Empower"**
24. **"Tap into"**
25. **"Capitalize on"**
26. **"Streamline"** - Without specific meaning
27. **"Optimize"** - Without specific meaning
28. **"Synergize"** - Without specific meaning

---

## Overpromising Words

29. **"Simply"**
30. **"Just"**
31. **"Easily"**
